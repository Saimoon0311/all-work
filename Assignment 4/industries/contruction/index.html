About Construction

The economic survey of Pakistan shows that the construction industry grew by 9.1% in FY17 and contributed 2.7% to the country’s gross domestic product (GDP) in that year.

The signing of the China-Pakistan Economic Corridor (CPEC) agreement and the gradual improvement in the country’s security situation have been imperative in boosting not only the construction industry locally but simultaneously improving the country’s image abroad as a potential, true regional player.
